Donations

The SHS Ski Team and the OHSNO are both non-profit entities that rely on donations to fund high school Nordic skiing in Oregon. Donations as small as $10 given to the SHS team can buy a tube of kickwax for the season and donations as large as $100 can buy a pair of skis that will be used for 5 or more seasons. Naming rights for races can be purchased through the OHSNO for as little as $350 for league races. This type of sponsorship benefits the racers in the league by reducing trail fees and providing revenue for purchasing timing equipment. If you are intersted in donating, please contact me and we can go over our options.
