Portfolio

I currently work as a Program Coordinator for Sisters Park and Recreation. I run sports leagues and events. Marquee events I have run include the Sisters Shootout, Crest the Cascades, and Sisters Annual Lacrosse Invitational. Additionally, I have a hand in marketing including creating our quarterly guide, newsletters, press releases, flyers, and other promotional pieces.

Along with my career, I am also the coach of the Sisters High School and Middle School Nordic team. To help run our statewide league, I am the Northern League representative for the Oregon Interscholastic Ski Racing Association.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring technology. Recent interests have included web site and web applications. I have an intermediate knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This webpage will serve as an outlet for many of these projects so check back to see what I've been creating!
